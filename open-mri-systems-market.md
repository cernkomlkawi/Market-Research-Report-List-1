<p><p>Open MRI systems are categorized by field strength, which affects image quality and patient experience. Low field strength (0.2-0.5 Tesla) systems prioritize comfort and ease of access, making them suitable for claustrophobic patients but offering lower resolution images. Medium field strength (0.5-1.5 Tesla) systems balance comfort with improved imaging quality, catering to a broader range of diagnostic needs. High field strength (1.5 Tesla and above) provides superior image clarity and detail, ideal for complex diagnoses, although they can be less patient-friendly.</p></p>
