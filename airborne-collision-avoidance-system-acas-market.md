<p><p>The Airborne Collision Avoidance System (ACAS) market comprises various systems designed to enhance aviation safety. ACAS I and TCAS I provide basic traffic advisories, helping pilots maintain safe distances. ACAS II and TCAS II are more advanced, offering resolution advisories for imminent threats. The Portable Collision Avoidance System (PCAS) is a smaller, adaptable solution for general aviation, while FLARM focuses on enhancing safety for lighter aircraft and gliders by detecting nearby traffic. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
