<p><p>The insulated garage door market primarily includes three types: polyurethane, polystyrene, and other materials. Polyurethane doors offer superior insulation due to their high-density foam core, resulting in enhanced energy efficiency and durability. Polystyrene doors feature a rigid foam core, providing moderate insulation and affordability, making them a popular choice for budget-conscious consumers. The "other" category encompasses alternative materials such as fiberglass and vinyl, which may offer varying levels of insulation, aesthetics, and maintenance needs, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
