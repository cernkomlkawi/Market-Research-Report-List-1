<p><strong>The Benchtop Dental Air Polisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasion Polishing</li><li>Erosion Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop dental air polisher market is categorized primarily into two types: abrasion polishing and erosion polishing. Abrasion polishing utilizes a combination of air and abrasive materials to effectively remove plaque and stains from teeth, delivering a smooth finish. In contrast, erosion polishing employs a gentler technique, using air and non-abrasive agents to enhance tooth surfaces without aggressive scrubbing. Both methods aim to improve dental aesthetics and hygiene, catering to various patient needs and preferences in dental care.</p></p>
