<p><p>An optical level switch is a device used to detect the presence or absence of liquid within a container or application. It operates using light transmission, where an optical sensor triggers an alarm or control signal when the liquid level changes, enabling precise monitoring and control of liquid processes. The advantages of optical level switches include their non-contact operation, resistance to contamination, and suitability for various liquids, making them ideal for industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage.</p><p>The Optical Level Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.
