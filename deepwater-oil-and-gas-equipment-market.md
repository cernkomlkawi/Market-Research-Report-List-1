The Wellhead System provides a surface connection for extracting oil and gas. Subsea Manifolds facilitate the flow of hydrocarbons, managing multiple wells. Connect Systems link subsea equipment to the surface, while Control Systems monitor and regulate operations. Umbilical Systems supply power and control signals to subsea installations. Other Systems include ancillary equipment critical for operational efficiency and safety in deepwater environments.</p></p>
