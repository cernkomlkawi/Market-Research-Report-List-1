<p><strong>The Wood and Furniture Paint Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood and furniture paint additives market is primarily divided into solventborne and waterborne types. Solventborne additives use organic solvents as their primary vehicle, offering excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. However, they may pose environmental and health risks. Waterborne additives, on the other hand, utilize water as the main solvent, resulting in lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup. They are becoming increasingly popular due to their eco-friendliness and safety while still providing good performance.</p></p>
