<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) Paste Resin market primarily involves two production methods: the micro suspension method and the emulsion method. The micro suspension method creates fine particle sizes, resulting in excellent clarity and flexibility, ideal for applications like coatings and sealants. In contrast, the emulsion method produces larger particles, yielding stronger and denser materials suitable for applications in construction and automotive industries. Both methods cater to diverse industries, driving demand for PVC paste resin based on desired properties and applications.</p></p>
