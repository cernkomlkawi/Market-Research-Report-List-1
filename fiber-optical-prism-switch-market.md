<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Switch</li><li>Multimode Fiber Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optical prism switch market comprises two main types: single-mode fiber switches and multimode fiber switches. Single-mode fiber switches are designed for long-distance communication over a single light path, offering low-loss transmission, making them ideal for telecommunication and data center applications. In contrast, multimode fiber switches are suited for shorter distances and can transmit multiple light modes simultaneously, facilitating high-speed data transfer in local area networks or within buildings. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs in modern networking solutions.</p></p>
