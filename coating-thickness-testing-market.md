<p><ul><li>Wet Film Thickness Checks</li><li>Dry Film Thickness Checks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coating thickness testing is essential for ensuring the quality and performance of protective coatings. The market comprises two main types: Wet Film Thickness Checks and Dry Film Thickness Checks. Wet Film Thickness Checks measure the thickness of a coating immediately after application, using tools like wet film gauges. In contrast, Dry Film Thickness Checks assess the thickness after the coating has cured, typically using magnetic or electronic gauges. Both methods are crucial for ensuring compliance with specifications and achieving optimal coating performance.</p></p>
