<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PCB Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Oven</li><li>Selective Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering system market consists of three primary types: wave soldering ovens, reflow ovens, and selective soldering ovens. </p><p>Wave soldering ovens utilize a wave of molten solder to connect components on a PCB, ideal for through-hole components. Reflow ovens are designed for surface-mount technology, heating solder paste to ensure proper connections through controlled temperature profiles. Selective soldering ovens combine both techniques, allowing for targeted soldering in complex PCB assemblies, making them versatile for mixed technology boards. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs.</p></p>
