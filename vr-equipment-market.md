<p><p>The VR equipment market comprises various types essential for immersive experiences. Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) provide visual immersion by displaying content directly in front of the user's eyes. Head Trackers monitor the orientation of the user's head for real-time perspective adjustments. Motion Trackers capture physical movement, enhancing interaction authenticity. 3D Controllers, like joysticks and gamepads, allow users to navigate virtual spaces. Data Gloves enable gesture-based control, while Haptic Devices simulate touch sensations. Additional products fall into the "Others" category, further expanding the market.</p></p>
