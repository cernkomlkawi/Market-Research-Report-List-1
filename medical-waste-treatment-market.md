<p><p>The medical waste treatment market primarily consists of incineration, autoclaves, and other methods. Autoclaves use steam and pressure to sterilize waste, effectively killing bacteria and viruses without incineration. Other treatments include chemical disinfection, microwaving, and land disposal.
