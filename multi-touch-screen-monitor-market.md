<p><p>The multi-touch screen monitor market offers various types, including capacitive, resistive, and sonic technologies. Capacitive screens detect touch through the electrical properties of the human body, providing high sensitivity and clarity. Resistive screens operate via pressure applied on flexible layers, making them more durable but with lower clarity and responsiveness. Sonic touch screens use sound waves to detect touch, offering unique advantages like high durability and the ability to work with gloves. Each type caters to specific user needs and applications.</p></p>
