<p><p>The Open Loop Hall Effect Current Sensor market can be divided into two main types: linear output and threshold output. Linear output sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the current being measured, allowing for precise current monitoring. In contrast, threshold output sensors generate a discrete signal that activates when the current exceeds a predefined level, making them ideal for overcurrent protection applications. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing electrical safety and efficiency in current measurement tasks.</p></p>
