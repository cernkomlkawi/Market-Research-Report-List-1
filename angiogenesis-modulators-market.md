<p><p>The angiogenesis modulators market is categorized into three main types: angiogenesis inhibitors, angiogenesis stimulators, and angiogenin, along with other agents. Angiogenesis inhibitors block the formation of new blood vessels, primarily used in cancer therapies to restrict tumor growth. Angiogenesis stimulators promote the development of new blood vessels, aiding in tissue repair and regeneration. Angiogenin, a specific factor involved in promoting blood vessel formation, plays a crucial role in various biological processes. Other agents may include novel compounds with angiogenic properties.</p></p>
