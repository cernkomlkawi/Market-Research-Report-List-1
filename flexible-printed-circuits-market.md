<p><p>The flexible printed circuits (FPC) market comprises three main types: single panel, double panel, and multilayer panel. Single panel circuits consist of a single layer of conductive material, ideal for simple applications. Double panels feature two conductive layers, enabling more complex circuitry and improving functionality. Multilayer panels consist of multiple layers of conductive material, allowing for advanced designs with high density and compactness. Each type serves varying needs across industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, driving market growth.</p></p>
