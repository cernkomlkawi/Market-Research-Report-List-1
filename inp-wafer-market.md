<p><ul><li>LEC Grown InP</li><li>VGF Grown InP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The InP wafer market consists of two primary types: LEC (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) grown InP and VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown InP. LEC grown InP utilizes a controlled crystallization process, ideal for high-purity and large-diameter wafers, catering primarily to optoelectronic applications. Conversely, VGF grown InP is characterized by its high production efficiency and suitability for various electronic applications. Both methods play crucial roles in advancing semiconductor technology, focusing on improving performance and scalability for diverse industries.</p></p>
