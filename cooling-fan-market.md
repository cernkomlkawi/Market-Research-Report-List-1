<p><strong>The Cooling Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling fan market is segmented into three main types: axial fans, centrifugal fans, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient airflow in single-direction applications, making them ideal for cooling electronic devices and ventilation. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, generate air movement by drawing air into the center and expelling it at a right angle, suitable for higher pressure applications like HVAC systems. The 'others' category encompasses specialized designs like blowing fans and multi-directional fans, catering to unique cooling needs.</p></p>
