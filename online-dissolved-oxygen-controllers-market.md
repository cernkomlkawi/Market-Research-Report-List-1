<p><p>The online dissolved oxygen controllers market can be categorized into three main types based on wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire systems. </p><p>2-wire controllers are simple and cost-effective, primarily transmitting power and signals through the same wires, making installation easy. </p><p>3-wire systems offer enhanced reliability by separating power and signal wires. </p><p>4-wire controllers provide the highest accuracy and are used in complex applications, allowing for more precise control and monitoring of dissolved oxygen levels in various environments.</p></p>
