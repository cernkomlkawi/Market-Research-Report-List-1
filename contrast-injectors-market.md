<p><ul><li>Orally</li><li>Rectally</li><li>Intravenous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contrast injectors market is segmented into three primary types: orally, rectally, and intravenously administered injectors. Orally administered contrast agents enhance imaging studies of the gastrointestinal tract, providing clarity for conditions like tumors or blockages. Rectal contrast is often used in specialized exams such as colonoscopy to visualize the lower digestive system. Intravenous contrast injectors are the most common, delivering contrast agents directly into the bloodstream for enhanced imaging of various organs, including the brain and heart, during procedures like CT and MRI scans.</p></p>
