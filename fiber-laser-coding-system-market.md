<p><p>The Fiber Laser Coding System market is primarily categorized into Pulse Fiber Laser Systems and Solid State Fiber Laser Systems. Pulse Fiber Laser Systems deliver high-energy light pulses suitable for marking and engraving applications, providing precision and quality over short durations. In contrast, Solid State Fiber Laser Systems generate continuous wave lasers, excelling in high-speed coding and industrial marking tasks. Both types enhance versatility in various industries, offering efficient solutions for product identification, batch coding, and traceability.</p></p>
