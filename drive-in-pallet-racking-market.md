<p><p>Drive-in pallet racking offers efficient storage solutions, optimizing warehouse space by allowing forklifts to load and retrieve pallets from the same side. There are three main types: Duplex Type enables two-sided access, maximizing storage capacity; Straight-Through Type allows pallets to enter and exit from opposite sides, facilitating smoother logistics; Single Entry Type restricts access to one side, ideal for smaller operations. Each type caters to different operational needs, enhancing efficiency and inventory management in warehouses.</p></p>
