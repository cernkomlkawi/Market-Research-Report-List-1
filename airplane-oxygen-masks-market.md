Passenger Type masks are designed for cabin use, deploying automatically in emergencies to supply oxygen when cabin pressure drops. These masks ensure passenger safety and comfort during in-flight emergencies. Conversely, Pilot Type oxygen masks are advanced, often featuring communication systems, and provide higher oxygen flow rates for flight crew, enabling them to maintain awareness and control during altitude crises. Both types are crucial for aviation safety.</p></p>
