<p><p>Single Photon Counting Modules (SPCM) are essential for detecting single photons and are classified into two main market types: Single Channel Counting Modules and Multi-Channel Counting Modules. Single Channel Counting Modules are designed for high sensitivity applications, providing reliable photon detection from a single channel. In contrast, Multi-Channel Counting Modules allow simultaneous detection across multiple channels, making them suitable for more complex experiments and applications where time resolution and spatial information are critical, enhancing overall performance in photon counting tasks.</p></p>
