<p><p>Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing) encompasses various technologies for creating objects layer by layer. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) uses a focused laser to fuse metal powder into dense parts, ideal for complex geometries and high-performance applications. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, offering advantages in speed and uniformity for larger components. Other market types include PolyJet, Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), and Stereolithography (SLA), each varying in materials and layer processes for diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
