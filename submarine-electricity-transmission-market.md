<p><p>The submarine electricity transmission market consists of three main cable types: mass-impregnated cables, extruded insulation cables, and others. Mass-impregnated cables are durable and suitable for high-voltage applications, utilizing a combination of cellulose and oil-based insulation. Extruded insulation cables feature solid polymer materials, offering greater flexibility and ease of installation for various voltage levels. The "other" category includes emerging technologies and specialized cables tailored for unique environmental or operational conditions, expanding the versatility of submarine power transmission solutions.</p></p>
