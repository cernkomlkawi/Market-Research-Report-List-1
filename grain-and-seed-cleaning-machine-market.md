<p><p>The grain and seed cleaning machine market consists of two primary types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning machines. Pre-cleaning machines remove larger impurities, such as straw and stones, preparing the grain for further purification. They typically use tools like screens and air blowers for initial separation. Fine cleaning machines focus on removing smaller impurities, including dust and broken grains, using advanced technologies like air classifiers and highly specialized sieves. Together, these machines enhance grain quality and ensure efficient processing in agricultural settings.</p></p>
