<p><ul><li>Direct-coupling</li><li>VAN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) solutions encompass two primary market types: direct-coupling and Value-Added Network (VAN) services. Direct-coupling involves a direct connection between trading partners' systems, allowing for real-time data exchange and efficiency without intermediaries. In contrast, VAN services act as intermediaries that facilitate the secure exchange of data between multiple partners, providing added features like data translation and storage. Both approaches serve to streamline communication and transaction processes in supply chain management and business operations.</p></p>
