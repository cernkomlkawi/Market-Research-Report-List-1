<p><strong>The Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbidimetric</li><li>Chromogenic</li><li>rFC Assays</li><li>Gel Clot</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endotoxin testing market includes various methods for detecting bacterial endotoxins. Turbidimetric assays measure the cloudiness of a solution caused by endotoxin-induced bacterial growth. Chromogenic assays utilize color change to indicate endotoxin presence. rFC assays, based on recombinant factor C, provide rapid and specific detection. Gel clot assays involve the formation of a gel in response to endotoxins. Additional methods may include molecular or alternative assays tailored for specific applications, catering to diverse industry needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
