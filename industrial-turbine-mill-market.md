<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Turbine Mill market is categorized primarily into vertical and horizontal mills. Vertical mills, characterized by their upright orientation, are known for effective airflow and better particle separation, making them suitable for fine grinding applications. In contrast, horizontal mills feature a side-to-side orientation, offering greater stability and efficiency for bulk processing needs. Both types are pivotal in various industries, such as food, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals, each serving specific operational requirements and material characteristics.</p></p>
