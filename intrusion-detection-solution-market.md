<p><p>The Intrusion Detection Solution market primarily encompasses two types: Network Intrusion Detection Systems (NIDS) and Host-based Intrusion Detection Systems (HIDS). NIDS monitor network traffic for suspicious activities by analyzing incoming and outgoing data packets, providing a broad view of network security. In contrast, HIDS focuses on individual hosts or devices, tracking system logs, file integrity, and user activities to detect anomalies. Together, these solutions enhance security by identifying and responding to potential threats in real time.</p></p>
