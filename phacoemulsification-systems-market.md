<p><p>The phacoemulsification systems market is categorized into three primary types: Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs), phacoemulsification equipment, and others. OVDs are crucial for maintaining anterior chamber stability and protecting corneal endothelium during cataract surgery. Phacoemulsification equipment includes machines that utilize ultrasonic energy to emulsify and aspirate lens material. The "others" category encompasses related accessories and consumables, such as surgical instruments and maintenance supplies, contributing to the overall efficiency and safety of cataract procedures.</p></p>
