<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Torque & Angle Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steering Mount Sensor</li><li>Steering Base Mount Sensor</li><li>Steering Column Mount Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering torque and angle sensor market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Steering Mount Sensor**: Located on the steering wheel, it measures the driver's input directly, ensuring precise feedback for enhanced control.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Steering Base Mount Sensor**: Positioned at the base of the steering column, this sensor gauges the angle and torque delivered to the steering system, crucial for stability and safety.</p><p>3. **Steering Column Mount Sensor**: Integrated within the steering column itself, it monitors steering dynamics, facilitating electronic stability control and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Each type plays a vital role in enhancing vehicle handling and safety.</p></p>
