<p><p>The IP network camera market is segmented into centralized and decentralized camera types. Centralized IP cameras rely on a central server for processing and storage, simplifying management but creating potential single points of failure. These systems are often used in larger setups where extensive surveillance is necessary. Decentralized IP cameras, on the other hand, have built-in storage and processing capabilities, allowing them to operate independently. This model enhances reliability and scalability, making it suitable for various applications, including smaller installations.</p></p>
