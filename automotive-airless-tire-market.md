<p><p>The automotive airless tire market includes two primary types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric arranged radially, providing better fuel efficiency, durability, and improved handling. They are popular in passenger vehicles for their performance advantages. On the other hand, bias tires have cords laid at angles, offering a sturdier construction that enhances load-carrying capacity. Bias tires are commonly used in heavy-duty applications due to their robustness but may sacrifice some performance compared to radial tires.</p></p>
